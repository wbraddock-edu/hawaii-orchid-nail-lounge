Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you'd like to know before your visit.

We strongly recommend booking ahead, especially for weekends and holidays. Our appointment slots fill quickly, particularly for nail art, bridal services, and spa pedicures. Same-day appointments are sometimes available — call us to check.
Gel polish is applied like traditional nail polish and cured under a UV/LED lamp. It's very flexible and comes in thousands of colors. Dip powder involves dipping nails into pigmented powder — it doesn't need UV curing and tends to be slightly stronger and longer-lasting than gel. Both last significantly longer than regular polish. We can help you decide which is right for your lifestyle during your consultation.
With proper application and care, gel manicures typically last 2–3 weeks. In Hawaii's conditions — swimming, humidity, sun exposure — you might see slight lifting at the edges before that. Following aftercare advice (moisturizing cuticles, wearing gloves for cleaning) significantly extends wear time.
Absolutely. Nail art is one of our specialties. For weddings, proms, quinceañeras, and other events, we recommend booking a consultation 4–6 weeks before your date so we can plan your design. Bring inspiration photos to your appointment — we love bringing your vision to life.
We use professional-grade products from OPI, Essie, CND, and Orly — all reputable brands that avoid the harshest chemicals. If you have known sensitivities or allergies, please let us know at booking. We can accommodate many skin conditions and will recommend the most appropriate products for your needs.
All metal tools are fully sterilized in an autoclave between clients. Single-use items like nail files, buffers, and toe separators are used once and discarded. We maintain the highest sanitation standards and have never received a health code violation since opening.
For bridal services, we recommend booking at least 4–6 weeks in advance for the service itself, and scheduling a design consultation before that. Weekend dates in June–August fill up months in advance. Call us as early as possible — we want to be part of your special day.
We ask for at least 24 hours notice for cancellations or rescheduling. Late cancellations (under 2 hours) and no-shows may be charged 50% of the service price. We understand that emergencies happen — please call us as soon as possible and we'll do our best to accommodate you.
