Nail Trends for Summer in Hawaii

📅 June 2025✍ Lily Nguyen⏱ 6 min read

Summer in Hawaii brings its own nail aesthetic — vibrant, playful, and inspired by the natural world surrounding us. Whether you're headed to the beach, a luau, a paddleboard session, or just living your best island life, your nails can reflect the beauty of the season. Here are the trends our clients are requesting most this summer at Orchid Nail Lounge.

Ocean-Inspired Chrome and Holographic

Aqua, teal, and ocean-chrome finishes are everywhere this summer. We're creating nails that capture the color of the Pacific at different times of day — deep navy for Hanauma Bay's depths, turquoise for shallow reefs, and holographic chrome that shifts like sunlight on water. These finishes photograph beautifully and work with literally every outfit.

Tropical Florals in Bold Colors

Hibiscus, plumeria, bird of paradise, and ti leaf designs continue to grow in popularity — but this season, our clients are requesting bolder, more graphic interpretations. Think hot pink hibiscus with thick black outlines on a white base, or single plumeria blooms on deeply-saturated colored backgrounds. Statement nail art that celebrates where we live.

Sunset Gradients

Maui, Oahu, and the Big Island give us some of the world's most spectacular sunsets — and this season we're wearing them. Warm gradient nails blending coral to orange to golden yellow capture that magic hour in a wearable format. These look especially beautiful with subtle shimmer added to the tips.

Simple Minimalism for Everyday

Not every day calls for statement nails, and minimalist aesthetics remain strong. Sheer pink nudes with a single accent nail, barely-there shimmer polishes, and clean white tips feel effortlessly chic. These styles are popular with our professional clients who want polished nails that suit both office and beach.
