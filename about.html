Our Story

Where Beauty Blooms

Lily Nguyen grew up watching her mother care for her hands with quiet pride — a small ritual of self-respect in a busy life. When Lily became a nail technician at 22, she carried that understanding with her: nail care isn't vanity, it's self-care. She opened Orchid Nail Lounge in Mililani in 2016 to give the community a place where that ritual could be elevated.

Today, Orchid employs five nail artists and a spa technician, all dedicated to the same vision: every client leaves feeling beautiful, refreshed, and genuinely cared for.
