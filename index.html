Our Story

A Little Luxury in Mililani

Orchid Nail Lounge was born from a vision — to bring genuine nail artistry and luxurious service to the families of Mililani. Founder Lily Nguyen opened the lounge in 2016 after a decade of training in both traditional and contemporary nail techniques, and has built a team of passionate artists who share her commitment to excellence.

From our premium non-toxic products to our relaxing spa environment, every detail of Orchid Nail Lounge is designed to make you feel beautiful, pampered, and at home.
